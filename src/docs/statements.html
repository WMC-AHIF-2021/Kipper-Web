  • Kipper Statements

    Statements form the basis of Kipper next to expressions, and can fundamentally change the flow of execution in your program. They can be easily identified with their usage of either curly brackets ({ }) or a semicolon (;).

    List of statements in Kipper

    Note that most of these statements will be explained in their own docs page!

    • Compound Statement

      A compound statement is also called block of code, and defines, as already explained in variables, a scope for variables. Compound statements are commonly used together with other statements, such as if-statements, while-loops and for-loops.
